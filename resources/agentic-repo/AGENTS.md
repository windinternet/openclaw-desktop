# AGENTS.md

This repository follows three rules:

1. Keep original facts in `sources/`; do not rewrite them unless correcting metadata.
2. Maintain synthesized knowledge in `wiki/`, especially `wiki/index.md` and `wiki/log.md`.
3. Move work through `work/`, `plans/`, `runs/`, `outputs/`, and `reviews/` with clear validation notes.

Start by reading `README.md`, `wiki/index.md`, `work/inbox.md`, and active plans under `plans/active/`.
